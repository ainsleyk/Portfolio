Simple Leaflet Routing

A simple Leaflet routing and geolocation example centered in Tacoma, WA. Use the marker in the upper right corner to zoom in on our genera location. Click anywhere on the map to add a start or end point to your route. You can add multiple stops to your route. This was built with Leaflet Routing Machine.
