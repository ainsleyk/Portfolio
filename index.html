Ainsley: Geospatial Communicator

Welcome to the home for my research, geopsatial analysis and visual desgin.

I work as a data scientist and communicator who specializes in geospatial analysis that aims for inclusive data collection and analysis. My use of effective visual communication and problem solving paired with diverse community engagement establish relationships built on trust, change, and understanding between government and the residents they aim to serve best.
